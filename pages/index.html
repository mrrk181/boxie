Boxie

Push your files.

Transfer your files safe and easily

Built using JavaScript, Node.js, Express, PureCSS, Dropzone, QCode Decoder, QRCode, multer, basic-auth

If you want to exchange some files between your computer and your smartphone, this is the tool for you.
You don't have to trust Dropbox or Google, just use Boxie. Read the source code on Github!
